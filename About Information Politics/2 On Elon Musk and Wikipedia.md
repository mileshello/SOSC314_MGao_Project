## Who is Elon Musk (in relation to this research project)
Elon Musk is a South African born entrepeneur living in the United States of America. Companies he currently leads include SpaceX, Tesla, and now X. Most of his companies include some focus on technological advancement, though they are also generally commercial.

Musk began showing support for the Republican party in the United States in 2022, the same year he acquired Twitter. His public reasoning for the acquisition was the support of the freedom of speech, as well as the pursuit of true information. He has since become very active politically, both on and off of the Twitter platform; he has appeared at political rallies in support of Donald Trump and other local republican political candidates.
